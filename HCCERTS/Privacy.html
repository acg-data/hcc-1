Privacy, Trust, and Data Security

Our commitment to your privacy

At HealthCareer Certs, we understand that trust is the foundation of education. We are committed to transparency, data security, and ensuring that your personal and academic information remains private and protected at all times.

How does HealthCareer Certs protect student data?

As a leader in online healthcare education, we have adopted industry-leading practices to ensure your personal and academic information is secure.

We're serious about protecting student information

  • We adhere to strict FERPA guidelines for academic records.
  • Your personal data is never sold to third-party advertisers.

We ensure schools maintain control over data

  • Partner schools and externship sites retain ownership of student records.
  • Transcripts are only released with your explicit consent.

We follow industry best practices

  • Our learning platform uses SSL encryption for all data transmission.
  • Payment gateways are fully PCI compliant to protect your financial info.

We continuously test and improve privacy

  • Regular security audits of our student portal.
  • We update our policies annually to match evolving digital standards.

Certifications & Compliance

HealthCareer Certs aligns its operations with national data privacy standards. Our platforms are built to ensure compliance with educational privacy laws, providing peace of mind for students, parents, and institutions.
