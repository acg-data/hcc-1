Who is eligible for MyCAA?

MyCAA is available to spouses of active duty, Guard, and Reserve members in specific pay grades. If your spouse falls into these ranks, you likely qualify for full tuition coverage.

  • 1
    No Cost: Covers tuition, exams, and books up to $4,000.
  • 2
    High School Diploma: Required to apply.

Careers that move with you

Military life means moving. Don't start your job search from scratch every time you PCS. Healthcare certifications are nationally recognized and in-demand everywhere.

  • Portable: Work in hospitals/clinics in any state.
  • High Demand: Allied health jobs are growing faster than average.

How to Apply

The process is simple, but we can help guide you through the MyCAA portal setup and career plan creation.

  • A
    Create Account: Log into MyCAA with your DS Logon.
  • B
    Create Plan: Choose Health Career Certs as your school.
  • C
    Get Approved: Receive your FA (Financial Assistance) document.
