Tuition & Financials

What's included in your program tuition?

Your tuition covers everything you need to succeed:

  • 1-on-1 Zoom Meetings: Direct support from instructors.
  • Interactive Online Classroom: Quizzes, flashcards, and study materials.
  • NHA Exam Prep: Comprehensive preparation for the national exam.
  • Guaranteed Externship: Placement in a local hospital or clinic.
How does "100% Tuition Refund" work?

If you don't land a job in the healthcare industry within 6 months of passing your exam and completing the externship, we will refund your tuition. Conditions apply: you must demonstrate active job searching, maintain communication with our career services, and not decline qualified job offers.

What Tuition Payment Options Are Available?

We offer two main options:

  1. Upfront Payment: Pay in full for maximum savings.
  2. Installment Plans: Pay in manageable monthly installments over the course of your class.

Academics & Exams

What's on the CCMA Exam?

The NHA CCMA exam consists of 150 scored questions (plus 30 pretest questions) over 3 hours. Here is the topic breakdown:

Topic Percentage
Clinical Patient Care56%
Foundational Knowledge10%
Care Coordination8%
Administrative Assisting8%
Communication8%
Medical Law & Ethics5%
Anatomy & Physiology5%
Can I get college credits after I pass my exam?

Yes! Through the American Council on Education (ACE), passing your exams can earn you credits transferrable to over 2,000 colleges:

  • CCMA: 5 ACE Credits
  • CPT (Phlebotomy): 2 ACE Credits
  • CPCT (Patient Care): 1 ACE Credit

Externships & Logistics

Do you guarantee externship placement?

Yes! We have a dedicated department that manages placements. We find a hospital or clinic convenient to your location for your hands-on training at no additional fee.

Are the training programs online or in-person?

All programs are 100% online for the didactic portion, allowing you to study at your own pace. This is followed by an optional (but recommended) hands-on externship at a local facility.
